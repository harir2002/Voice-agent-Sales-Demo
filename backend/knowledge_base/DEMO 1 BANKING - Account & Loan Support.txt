EMI CALCULATION
Formula: EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1]
Example: ₹5,00,000 at 12% for 60 months = ₹11,100 per month
Total Interest: ₹1,66,000
Online EMI Calculator: www.bank.com/emi-calculator

KYC REQUIREMENTS
Mandatory: Aadhaar, PAN, Address Proof, Photo
Income Proof: Salary Cert or Last 3 months bank statements
Processing: 2-3 business days
Verification: Background check + CIBIL review
Update Process: Visit branch or use Mobile Banking App > Services > Update KYC

ACCOUNT SERVICES
Account Balance: Check via App, Net Banking, ATM, SMS

Monthly Charges: ₹100-₹500 if balance <₹5,000
Account Statement: Monthly or on-demand
Minimum Balance: ₹5,000 (Savings), ₹25,000 (Current)
Joint Account: Available for family members, requires KYC for both holders

FAQ - BANKING
Q: How do I check my loan balance? A: Use Mobile App > Loan Details, or call customer care.
Q: Can I prepay my loan? A: Yes, after 12 months. 1% penalty applies.
Q: What's my CIBIL score? A: Free report at www.cibil.com. Scores >750 get better rates.
Q: How long does approval take? A: Personal/Auto: 2-5 days. Home: 15-30 days.
Q: How do I open a savings account? A: Apply online with Aadhaar/PAN or visit nearest branch.
Q: Where is the nearest ATM? A: Use the 'Locator' feature in our Mobile App.
Q: How can I get a cheque book? A: Request via Net Banking or Mobile App. Delivered in 5-7 days.
Q: Do you have safe deposit lockers? A: Yes, available at select branches. Annual fee ₹2000-₹10000.
