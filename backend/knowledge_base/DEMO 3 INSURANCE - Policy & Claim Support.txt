INSURANCE PRODUCTS

TERM LIFE INSURANCE
Coverage: ₹25,00,000 to ₹1,00,00,000
Premium: ₹200-₹1,000 per month (depends on age, health)
Tenure: 10, 20, 30 years
Claim Settlement: 7 days (if no complications)
Eligibility: Age 18-65, medical checkup may required
Difference vs Life Insurance: Term is pure protection (no maturity benefit). Life/Endowment has savings component.

HEALTH INSURANCE
Coverage: ₹5,00,000 to ₹25,00,000
Premium: ₹300-₹2,000 per month (depends on age, coverage)
Network Hospitals: 10,000+ across India
Claim Settlement: Cashless at network hospitals. Reimbursement elsewhere.
Pre-existing Disease: Covered after 1-4 years waiting period.
Dental Treatment: Usually NOT covered unless due to accident/hospitalization.
Family Floater: Add spouse/children/parents to same plan.
Maternity: Covered in specific plans with 9-24 month waiting period.

MOTOR INSURANCE
Third-Party: Mandatory, covers damage to others' vehicle/property.
Comprehensive: Covers own damage + third-party + theft + natural calamities.
Premium: ₹2,000-₹8,000 annually (depends on vehicle, age, IDV).
No Claim Bonus (NCB): 20-50% discount on renewal premium for claim-free years.
Add-ons: Zero Depreciation (bumper-to-bumper), Engine Protect, Roadside Assistance.

HOME & TRAVEL INSURANCE
Home: Covers building + contents against fire, theft, flood. Premium ₹3k-15k/year.
Travel: Medical emergency, flight delay, baggage loss. Premium ₹500/trip. Instant issuance.

POLICY MANAGEMENT
Premium Payment: Monthly, quarterly, annual (annual saves 5% more). Grace period 15-30 days.
Renewal: Online via app/website. Renew before expiry to save NCB.
Portability: Can switch insurer at renewal without losing benefits (apply 45 days prior).
Cancellation: Free look period 15 days. Pro-rata refund thereafter.
Nominee: Change details via 'Service Request' in app.

CLAIM PROCESS
Step 1: Report claim within 24 hours (call 1800-456-7890 or App).
Step 2: Provide documents (Medical: bills, discharge summary; Motor: FIR, photos, license).
Step 3: Assessment by surveyor/TPA.
Step 4: Approval & settlement (Cashless: Direct to hospital/garage; Reimbursement: 15-30 days).
Claim Settlement Ratio: 98.5% (High reliability).

FAQ - INSURANCE
Q: How do I file a claim? A: Call 1800-456-7890 or use app > Claim section. Provide required documents within 15 days.
Q: How long does claim settlement take? A: 7-30 days depending on policy and documentation. Health claims faster at network hospitals.
Q: Can I renew my expired policy? A: Yes, within 30 days of expiry (grace period). Coverage stops during this gap.
Q: What is a cashless claim? A: Insurer pays hospital directly. You only pay non-medical expenses.
Q: Can I increase coverage? A: Yes, at the time of renewal (Top-up plan).
Q: How do I download policy? A: Log in to portal > My Policies > Download PDF.
