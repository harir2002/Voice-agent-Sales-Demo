COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS

BILLING & SUBSCRIPTION
- Double Charge: Contact support with transaction ID. Refund in 7-10 days.
- Refund Not Received: Check bank statement after 10 days. Contact bank with ARN number.
- Change Plan: Account Settings > Subscription > Change Plan. Prorated charge applies.
- Cancel Subscription: Account Settings > Subscription > Cancel. Valid till period end.
- Update Billing Address: Settings > Billing > Edit Address.

ACCOUNT & LOGIN
- Forgot Password: Click "Forgot Password" > Enter Email > Reset link sent.
- Username: Usually your email address. Contact support if forgotten.
- Account Locked: Unlocks auto after 30 mins. Or reset password to unlock immediately.
- Delete Account: Settings > Privacy > Delete Account. Irreversible after 30 days.

PRODUCT & SERVICE
- Internet Slow: Restart router. Check service outage map on website.
- Damaged Product: Report within 48 hours with photos. Return pickup arranged.
- Wrong Item: Keep item unused. Initiate return > Reason "Wrong Item".
- Warranty: Standard 1 year. Extended warranty available for purchase within 30 days.
- User Manual: Available in 'Resources' section of website.
- Promo Code: Apply at checkout. Check validity and T&C.

ORDER MANAGEMENT
- Track Order: Use tracking ID sent via SMS/Email on courier website.
- Cancel Order: Allowed before shipping. Instant refund to source.
- Delivery Time: Standard 5-7 days. Express 2-3 days.
- Missing Package: Mark as "Not Received" in app. Investigation takes 48 hours.
