HEALTHCARE DEMO 2 - DOCUMENT PACK
Healthcare Sector RAG Document - Patient Support, Medical History, Prescriptions

MEDICAL RECORDS ACCESS
Access: Log in to Patient Portal or App.
History: View past visits, diagnosis, and treatment plans.
Download: All reports available as PDF.
Share: "Share with Doctor" feature available. Or download and email.
Confidentiality: HIPAA compliant. Access only to you and treating doctor.
Emergency Contact: Update in Profile > Emergency Info.

LAB RESULTS & REPORTS
Availability: Blood tests (24h), X-ray/Scan (48h).
Notification: SMS/Email alert when report is ready.
View Online: Portal > Reports > Lab/Radiology.
Hard Copy: Collect from reception if needed.
Interpretation: Doctor will discuss in follow-up. Normal ranges shown in report.

PRESCRIPTIONS & MEDICATIONS
Current Meds: View active prescriptions in App.
Refill: Click "Request Refill" for chronic meds. Doctor approval in 4 hours.
Missed Dose: Consult doctor. Generally, take as soon as remembered unless close to next dose.
Side Effects: Report immediately via "Message Doctor" or call clinic.
Physiotherapy: Prescription available for sessions. Book via app.

HEALTH & LIFESTYLE
Vaccination History: View past vaccines and due dates in "Immunization" tab.
Diet Chart: Personalized diabetic/BP diet charts available on request.
Vitals Tracking: Log BP, Sugar, Weight in App to share with doctor.
BMI: Auto-calculated based on height/weight data.
Symptoms: Flu (Fever, cough, body ache). Dengue (High fever, joint pain).

GENERAL SUPPORT
Discharge Summary: Available in records section post-hospitalization.
Medical Certificate: Request during consultation or via portal (charges apply).
Allergies: Listed in profile. Important to update if new allergies found.
Chronic Pain: Pain management specialist available.
Surgery History: All past procedures performed here are recorded.

FAQ - PATIENT RECORDS
Q: Can I get my recent lab results? A: Yes, check the 'Reports' section in the app.
Q: I need a prescription refill. A: Use the 'Refill Request' button for active prescriptions.
Q: Show me my vaccination history. A: Go to Health Profile > Vaccinations.
Q: How do I update my emergency contact? A: Profile > Edit > Emergency Contact.
Q: Can I get a diet chart? A: Yes, ask your doctor or nutritionist to assign one to your profile.
